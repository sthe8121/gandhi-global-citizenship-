Gandhi’s Influence Worldwide

Martin Luther King Jr. – Adopted nonviolence in the US Civil Rights Movement.
Nelson Mandela – Embraced Gandhian ideals during South Africa’s anti-apartheid struggle.
César Chávez – Used peaceful protest in US labor movements.

Key Events

1930: Salt March – Protested British salt monopoly.
1942: Quit India Movement – Demanded end to British rule.
1947: Indian Independence – Achieved freedom; Gandhi advocated peace.
